Transfer Learning Program

Unlock Your Full Potential

This program is designed to support ambitious Bocconi students in reaching their full potential. If selected, you’ll be matched with a senior BSML member active in your field of interest. Your mentor will offer guidance, share insights, and may even provide referrals. They’ve stood where you stand—they studied at Bocconi and have gone on to follow the paths you aspire to.

Whether you aim for top universities for a Master’s or PhD, or a career in quantitative finance or data science, our mentors can help. Explore the areas below and apply now.

APPLY TO THE PROGRAM
Quantitative Finance Industry
Interested in a career in quantitative finance, specifically on the buy-side? We’re here to support you every step of the way:
  • Mock interviews
  • Access to prep materials for brainteasers, mental math, and coding challenges
  • Personalised CV reviews for quant finance roles
Candidates who build a strong connection with their mentor may become eligible for a referral.
Data Science Industry
Applying to data science jobs? Our mentors support you with:
  • Mock interviews
  • Tailored preparation strategies
  • CV reviews focused on data science roles
Strong candidates may also receive a referral.
Machine Learning Research Research
Have a machine learning project? We can help you:
  • Develop and refine your project
  • Review ideas and methodology
  • Get feedback from peers
Master’s Academia
Thinking about pursuing a Master’s abroad? Our mentors can guide you through applications, SOPs, recommendations, and interviews.
PhD Academia
Considering a PhD abroad? Mentors can help you with applications, statements of purpose, recommendation letters, and insights from their experience.
