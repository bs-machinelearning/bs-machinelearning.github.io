Tech Research in the Product Era

  • Room N07, Velodromo Building
  • May 7, 2025

During our last event co-organized with Hephaestus AI, we brought together legal, technical, and academic perspectives to explore how tech research becomes real-world technology—and how regulation and ethics shape that journey.

A huge thank you to our speakers Guenda Sciancalepore (Cloud Solution Architect at Microsoft) and Veronica M. Pruinelli (lawyer specialized in AI regulation). We discussed:

  • How research teams work and deliver impact
  • How to translate AI ideas into AI products
  • The balance between innovation and regulation
  • Ethical challenges of deploying AI at scale

At BSML, we aim to connect minds, not only layers—thank you for being part of the conversation.
